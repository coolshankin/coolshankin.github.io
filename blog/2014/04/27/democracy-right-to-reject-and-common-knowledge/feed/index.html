On NOTA:
Nota can opted for the following reasons
-I want to reject all candidates
-I do not want to vote and my vote should not be misused by others
-I do not want to participate in this election (some areas boycotted the election process)
-I am not interested in this democratic process

A voter presses NOTA button intentionally, that means it not INVALID.
It is valid and tells the Election commission to take care of the election process in a better way. Hence it is a powerful option for voter. If NOTAs are more, political parties have to correct themselves and governance to be improved.
Instead of boycotting the election process, opt for NOTA and thereby attract political parties’ attention.
